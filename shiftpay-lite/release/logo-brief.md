# ClockRoster Logo Brief

## Brand Direction
A clean, trustworthy HR operations brand.

## Concept Options
1. Clock + Grid
- Circular clock outline + 7-cell roster grid
- Communicates time + scheduling

2. CR Monogram
- Stylized C/R with a check mark
- Communicates approval/workflow completion

3. Pin Clock
- Small location pin merged with clock hand
- Communicates clock-in with location context

## Visual Style
- Minimal, flat, modern
- Works at small icon sizes (App Store icon constraints)

## Color Directions
- Primary: #4F7CFF
- Accent: #23C16B
- Dark background friendly

## App Icon Guidance
- Strong center glyph
- No tiny text in icon
- High contrast edge for visibility at 60x60
